About Our Neighborhood

We are a subdivision in Canton, Michigan comprised of 309 single family residential homes intertwined with the Pheasant Run Golf Course, neighboring subdivisions and the Canton Township municipal complex and park system. Our association is responsible for the maintenance of the landscaping, irrigation, snow management, common utilities and general business with the common areas and we are a member of the PRRMA board.
The Pheasant Run Roadway Manager Association (“PRRMA”) is an oversight board that is comprised of 5 members (2 township officials and 1 from each of the 3 member HOAs) that is responsible for the management of the entire roadway system (ROW) throughout Fairway Pines at Pheasant Run Subdivision, Fairways at Pheasant Run Subdivision, Pheasant Run Subdivision and Summit Parkway.
As a homeowner in our subdivision you are a member of the association and have rights and responsibilities as part of the organization. All homeowners, especially newer residents, are encouraged to review our bylaws & covenants.
