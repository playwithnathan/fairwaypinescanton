Welcome Home

Welcome not only to the neighborhood and your new home but to your local Homeowners Association. Our Homeowners Association is run by the local Board of Directors. The association is also governed by a set of documents including the Covenants and Restrictions (C&Rs) and the Bylaws. The association's primary responsibility is to protect and enhance the health, safety and welfare of its members. The Board has a fiduciary responsibility to make decisions that protect your investments, your property, and the common areas of our community.

Waste Removal

Curbside pick-up of residential yard waste begins early in the spring until early winter. Exact dates vary yearly and are published in the Canton Township Focus Newsletter. All yard waste and recyclables should be placed separately from your trash on your regular collection day. Containers bigger than 32 gallon capacity will not be accepted and all containers should weigh under 50 pounds. Acceptable yard waste items include: grass clippings, leaves, pruning debris, wood debris and brush. All containers to be marked "YARD WASTE" Unacceptable yard waste items include: food scraps, animal waste, batteries, chemicals, sod, dirt, clay Stickers for reusable containers are available at the Canton Treasurer's office or by calling GFL Enviromental @ (586) 772-8900.

Architectural Guidelines

Any modification or new construction to the home or outside of the home require an approved Architecture Review and should be communicated and approved by the Board of Directors, prior to beginning construction.
